What is Philosophy?

Philosophy is the study of fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, values, reason, mind, and language. It is a quest for understanding and wisdom, engaging with complex questions and problems that have intrigued humanity for millennia.

Branches of Philosophy

Philosophy encompasses several branches, each focusing on different aspects of the human experience:

  • Metaphysics: The study of existence and reality.
  • Epistemology: The study of knowledge and belief.
  • Ethics: The study of right and wrong conduct.
  • Logic: The study of valid reasoning and argument.
  • Aesthetics: The study of beauty, art, and taste.
